I was tasked with not only updating the branding on the homepages for the Ark Encounterlink and Creation Museumlink sister attractions, but also finding UX solutions to display the current operating hours of the attractions and to allow space for announcements.

My solution was to add a new section below the hero photo to house our most important info. The first section was reserved for the ticketing CTA. It was important for that to be above the fold on all devices. The second section is optional, and allows space for any announcements (currently our Covid-19 health info). The third section was the perfect place for our hours. This placement was much more ideal since hours only appeared in the footer previously.
